# Department Head Portal

> Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Overview

The Department Head Portal manages academic activities within a single department.

Unlike the Admin Portal, which controls the entire university, the Department Head Portal focuses only on one department and supervises faculty members, course offerings, academic progress, and result approval.

Each Department Head can access only their assigned department.
