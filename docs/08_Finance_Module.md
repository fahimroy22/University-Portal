# Finance Module

> Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Overview

The Finance module manages all student financial activities.

Unlike traditional systems, every student's complete payment history remains attached to their account throughout their academic life.

The module tracks

- Semester Fees
- Payments
- Waivers
- Receipts
- Due Balances
- Admit Card Eligibility
